History of the Song

"Sweet Child O' Mine" is a song by the American rock band Guns N' Roses, released in 1987 on their debut album "Appetite for Destruction". The song was written by the band's lead guitarist Slash, lead singer Axl Rose, and rhythm guitarist Izzy Stradlin. Axl Rose wrote the lyrics inspired by his then-girlfriend Erin Everly. The song became one of the band's most famous hits, reaching number one on the Billboard Hot 100 and remaining a staple of rock radio.
